About Hon. Naomi Nzioki

Hon. Naomi Nzioki's leadership is built on the values of progress, unity, and inclusion, ensuring that every voice in our community is heard and represented.

With years of experience in public service, she has worked tirelessly to address key issues such as education, health, food and shelter to improve the quality of life for all residents through the The Naomi Get Together Foundation.

Our Key Factors

Hon. Naomi Nzioki’s priorities for a better tomorrow

Accessible Education

By equipping learning centers, supporting teachers, equally distributing bursaries and providing free learning materials, we’ll ensure every child gets equal opportunities to succeed.

Youth and Sports

Youth unemployment is tough right now, hitting our community hard. I’m committed to delivering real jobs and opportunities so that our young people have a future they can count on. I will partner with the Ministry of Lands, Public Works, and Urban Development to create tangible opportunities for the skilled workforce in Mumbuni North ward in Machakos County .

Affordable Healthcare

We plan to improve community health centers, launch mobile clinics, and support maternal and child health programs in rural areas.

Our Manifesto

Hon. Naomi Nzioki’s vision for a brighter, united community.

Creating job opportunities

We will engage youth in innovation hubs and we will also provide financial literacy programs, start-up grants, and vocational training for young people to build a self-reliant generation.

Supporting local businesses and traders.

We’ll empower farmers, traders, and small businesses through low-interest loans, capacity building, and market access programs.

Environment & Sustainability

Promoting green energy and clean communities. We’ll plant more trees, support waste recycling programs, and push for solar-powered schools and public offices.
